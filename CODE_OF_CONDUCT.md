# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We want this project to be a welcoming, constructive place to discuss Home
Assistant, Codex, and the software around them. Everyone participating in the
project is expected to act with respect, curiosity, and care for other people's
time and privacy.

## Expected behaviour

- Be considerate in technical disagreement and focus feedback on ideas and
  changes rather than people.
- Assume good intent, ask for clarification, and make room for different levels
  of experience.
- Protect private information. Do not post credentials, device codes, tokens,
  workspace contents, or identifying Home Assistant details.
- Accept responsibility for mistakes, correct them where practical, and help
  keep discussions useful and on topic.

## Unacceptable behaviour

Harassment, discrimination, threats, personal attacks, deliberate disruption,
unwanted disclosure of private information, and other conduct that makes
participation unsafe or hostile are not acceptable. This applies in issues,
pull requests, discussions, and project-maintained communication spaces.

## Enforcement

Report a conduct concern privately to the maintainer through the repository's
GitHub profile. Reports will be handled discreetly and in good faith. The
maintainer may request clarification, remove content, limit participation, or
take another proportionate action to protect the community.

For a potential security vulnerability, use the private process in
[SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) instead of this route.
